 Please join us to celebrate One Hope United’s 117-year history of protecting children and strengthening families with this inaugural fashion event. 

Saturday, April 28, 2012
7 – 11 p.m.

Mercedes-Benz of Chicago
1520 W. North Ave
Chicago, IL, 60642 (Map)

April is Child Abuse Prevention month. We are gathering to raise awareness and funds to support One Hope United’s efforts to prevent these horrific events from happening, or happening again.

Host Committee
Benjamin Cottrell--Benjamin Cottrell Designs
Delrice Adams--Forecast for the Arts
Glenn Murray--220 Communications
Jenaeth Markaj--One Hope United
Larry James--House of Trend
Marco Foster--Chicago Models
Sarah Vargo--Maven

Come dressed in the style of your favorite era! Flappers, hippies, children of the ‘80s, fashionistas from the future…all are welcome! Capture your look in the party photo booth!

Fabulous fashion installations produced by Benjamin Cottrell Designs will feature looks created by four designers, with hair and makeup by Strange Beauty Show and Lora Michael.

Fashion and music will change hourly, starting with the 1920s and moving through the eras.

* Entertainment and dancing

* Music by David Fenmore.

* Bites and beverages

* Premium goodie bags

* Silent auction

* Complimentary one-year subscription to Chicago Magazine


Admission: $40 in advance; $50 at the door
